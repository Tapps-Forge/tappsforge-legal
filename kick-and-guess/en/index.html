KICK & GUESS

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 5, 2026 Last updated: April 5, 2026

Summary

Kick & Guess is a hangman-style football trivia game developed by TaPps Forge. We built it to be as private as possible — it works completely offline, stores all data locally on your device, and requires no account.

This policy explains what data is collected, why, and how it is managed.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal identifier
  • We have no user accounts or sign-up process
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • Game progress, lives, streaks, and settings are stored solely on your device

Data collected by third-party services

Kick & Guess integrates the following third-party SDKs that may independently collect data under their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob

Displays interstitial and rewarded ads to free users. It may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data for contextual or personalized advertising.

policies.google.com/privacy →

RevenueCat

Manages in-app purchases and Pro unlock validation. It collects purchase receipts and device identifiers to verify and restore purchases.

revenuecat.com/privacy →

Local notifications

Kick & Guess may send local notifications to remind you about the daily challenge. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and are not sent to any server. You can disable them at any time from the app Settings or your device Settings.

Purchases

The Kick & Guess Pro unlock is a one-time purchase processed by Google Play. Payment data is handled entirely by the platform and never by TaPps Forge. RevenueCat is used solely to verify and restore the purchase status.

Minimum age requirement

Kick & Guess is intended for users aged 13 and older. The app content is suitable for all ages (PEGI 3), but we restrict access to ages 13+ due to the inclusion of advertising served by Google AdMob, in compliance with Google Play advertising policies.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a minor has accessed or provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.
